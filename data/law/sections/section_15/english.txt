Section 15.   Special Public Prosecutor and Exclusive Public Prosecutor.

1 [15. Special Public Prosecutor and Exclusive Public Prosecutor.--(1) For every Special Court, the State Government shall, by notification in the Official Gazette, specify a Public Prosecutor or appoint an advocate who has been in practice as an advocate for not less than seven years, as a Special Public Prosecutor for the purpose of conducting cases in that Court.
(2) For every Exclusive Special Court, the State Government shall, by notification in the Official Gazette, specify an Exclusive Special Public Prosecutor or appoint an advocate who has been in practice as an advocate for not less than seven years, as an Exclusive Special Public Prosecutor for the purpose of conducting cases in that Court.]



1. Subs. by s.10, ibid., for section 15 (w.e.f. 26-1-2016)
